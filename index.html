On Love...

Love is not limited by anything. Love knows no gender. But even so, a lot of LGBTQ people are still experiencing discrimination. It causes real harm, many have experienced bullying and violence just because they are being true to themselves.

Who are the LGBTQ?

Lesbian

Lesbians are women who love other women.

Gay

Gays are men who love other men.

Bisexual

Bisexuals are people attracted to more than one gender.

Transgender

Transgenders are people who identifies as a gender different from the one they were assigned at birth.

Queer

Queer is often used by people who don't fit traditional labels.

The Key...

Acceptance is the key that we need in order to build a more humane society wherein people are free to show their true authentic self, no matter what color their flag are. Everyone deserves to be treated equal, no matter who they are and who they love.

Acceptance

Gender is not the basis of worth, but the heart, mind, and deeds. So let us make a world where everyone lives without the fear of being judged. Respect is not something that we should beg for, it is given without any exception.
